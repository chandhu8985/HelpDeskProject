Welcome to HelpDesk

At Help desk , we understand the challenges faced by office staff in managing requests efficiently. Our platform is designed to streamline the process, making it easier than ever to handle office-related tasks seamlessly. What You Can Do with Help desk :

1. Raise a New Request

Empower yourself to initiate new requests effortlessly. Our intuitive interface guides you through submitting requests with clarity and speed. You can raise a new request by clicking the button "New Request" on the top right , after you login.It will take you to a form to fill the required details fill accordingly and submit the request.

2. View History of Requests

Access a comprehensive archive of all your past requests. Review previous resolutions, and timelines to aid future decision-making and reporting. You can view the history of requests in the home page , right after you login. The history list displays various coloumns, which give detailed infromation of the requests.

3. See the Status of Raised Requests

Stay informed every step of the way. Track the progress of your requests in real-time. Know when it's being open, in progress, and completed, ensuring you're always in the loop. You can see the staus in the history of requests. There is a coloumn named "status" in the list to check your current status.

4. Cancel Requests

Need to cancel a request? No problem. Our platform allows you to manage your requests dynamically. Simply click "Delete" when necessary, ensuring your workflow remains flexible and efficient. The delete button is found on every unsolved request in the history, click on the delete button to cancel that request.

5. Update a Request

Keep your requests up-to-date with ease. Whether it's providing additional information, or clarifying details, our platform enables seamless communication and updates throughout the lifecycle of your request. You can click on "edit" button, it is found on every request in the history. It will prompt you to edit the details of that request.Update the required fields and submit again. It will update your request details.

6. View Specific Request Details.

A request's specific details can be viewed by clicking on the "id" in the history list.It will give a pop-up, which contains all details about the request.
